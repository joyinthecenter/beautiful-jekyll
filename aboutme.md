Gail Swanson founded Haymarket Studio after 25 years in technology and public service. Focused on making technology that feels good to use and helps ease the demands of daily life. Gail is an information architect that enjoys the making of things as much as figuring out the what and how to solve problems. 

Through experience guiding teams from uncertainty to clarity, she has developed diverse techniques to adapt to nearly any situation. Gail works with teams focused on public good, and small business owners contributing to their communities.
